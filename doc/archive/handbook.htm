1. Wire assignments

Wire No. Purpose
1-5 General use
6-10 Traffic handling
11 US dialup hub
12 Cdn dialup hub
13 Dot code
14 Teletype
15 International Morse
16-100 Unassigned
101-129 Continuous broadcasts

If you want to use a particular wire for some purpose but it happens to be busy, then choose the next available wire number.  For example, if you want to chat with someone but the No. 1 wire  is in use, then try wire number 2, 3, 4, or 5.  Similarly, if you have message traffic that you want to handle and the No. 6 wire is busy, then use the No. 7 wire, etc.

3. Office call roster

The following office calls have been registered for use with the KOB system.  To make a correction or register a new office call, please send an email to MorseKOB-owner@yahoogroups.com.  Newcomers are always welcome on the KOB system, whether you have a registered call or not.

Duplicate office calls are listed in order of seniority.  That is, the office call registered first appears higher in the list than an office with the same call that was registered later.  In case two offices with the same call happen to be on the same wire at the same time, the office with less seniority is expected to defer to the senior office and temporarily use a different office call.
